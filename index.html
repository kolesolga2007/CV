About

 

I am a member of Natural Language Processing Laboratory at the Center for Computing Research (Centro de Investigación en Computación, CIC) of the National Polytechnic Institute (Instituto Politécnico Nacional, IPN) Mexico City, Mexico

My research interests in natural language processing are sentiment analysis, opinion mining, machine translation, syntactic and semantic knowledge representation in language models. Visit my current projects page and publications page for more details. 

I teach courses at graduate level in natural language processing. 

Study at our Center: Apply for our PhD and Master Programs in Computer Science with scholarship from the National Council for Humanities, Science, and Technology (CONAHCYT), a governmental agency in Mexico.
